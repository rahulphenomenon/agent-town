# Attribution

Paperclip Office currently uses code-drawn placeholder geometry and UI, so no third-party sprite sheets or raster assets are bundled in this worktree yet.

The feature design and interaction direction draw inspiration from:

- `paperclipai/paperclip` for the underlying control-plane model and tracker routes
- `geezerrrr/agent-town` for spatial office interaction ideas
- `pablodelucca/pixel-agents` for office ambience and character-status inspiration
- `a16z-infra/ai-town` for playful management-sim framing
- WorkAdventure documentation for top-down office map conventions

If third-party visual assets are added later, record the exact source path, license, and modifications here before shipping.
